Karthik Surya is an Indian YouTuber and television anchor from Trivandrum, India. Karthik Surya, an eminent figure in the Indian entertainment scene, stands as a beacon of innovation and creativity in the realm of digital content creation. Hailing from the culturally rich region of Trivandrum, Kerala, Karthik has carved a distinctive niche for himself as a multifaceted personality, adorning the roles of both a prolific YouTuber and a charismatic television anchor. His journey into the realm of digital storytelling commenced in 2011 when he launched his YouTube channel, laying the foundation for what would soon burgeon into a thriving platform for lifestyle vlogging. However, it was not until 2017 that Karthik ventured into the realm of vlogging, marking a significant turning point in his career trajectory. Fuelled by an insatiable passion for content creation and a relentless pursuit of his dreams, Karthik took the bold leap to transition from his role as a business development manager at Technopark to embrace the exhilarating realm of full-time vlogging.

In 2021, Karthik's meteoric rise to prominence reached new heights when he was bestowed with the prestigious opportunity to helm the Mazhavil Manorama programme, "Oru Chiri Iru Chiri Bumper Chiri," further cementing his status as a prominent figure in the television landscape. Renowned as the trailblazer of lifestyle vlogging in Kerala, Karthik's YouTube channel serves as a captivating tapestry of his adventures, insights, and experiences, captivating the hearts and minds of millions of viewers worldwide. With an impressive subscriber base exceeding 2.27 million and a staggering view count surpassing 330,002,460 as of March 2023, Karthik continues to captivate audiences with his unique blend of authenticity, charm, and relatability, leaving an indelible mark on the digital landscape.
